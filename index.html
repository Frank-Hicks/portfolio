*Welcome Mat*

Notice:

2018 graduate of Texas A&M University's Visualization Department, my focus was primarily in Visual and Interactive Design. The Viz Department provided a great platform for learning, exposure to the expansive landscape of design and laid the groundwork for the eventual path I am now attempting to build.

While there, I had the opportunity to be involved with projects in branding and print design, UI/UX and system design, as well as serve in both the Social Media Manager and Vice President roles of the design organization, AIGA TAMU.

Post-graduation, I've worked primarily as a graphic designer and commercial photo editor but have had a creative or technical hand in projects ranging from product photography, social media and advertising to identity and apparel design.
